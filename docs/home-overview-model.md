# Home Overview Model

The home radar compares active goals only. Completed goals are intentionally removed from the radar and kept on the Completed Goals page with their history intact.

## Current Mode

Current mode answers: "How healthy is this goal in the selected tracking period?"

- Daily tasks score by completions in the selected Sunday-to-Saturday week divided by 7.
- Weekly tasks score as complete or incomplete for the selected week.
- Custom tasks score by progress toward their selected weekly or monthly target.
- One-off tasks are excluded because they do not represent ongoing consistency.

## All-Time Trend Mode

All-time trend answers: "How consistent has this goal been since it started accumulating recurring completions?"

- Daily tasks score against elapsed calendar days from the first recurring completion through the selected date.
- Weekly tasks score against elapsed expected weeks over that same span.
- Custom tasks score against elapsed expected weekly or monthly targets.
- Sparse new history can move quickly. That is acceptable for now because the mode is meant to explain long-term average consistency, not forecast future performance.

## Product Stance

Keep radar as the primary comparison view for now. The important distinction is recent period health versus long-term consistency. If future users find radar hard to interpret, that should become a visualization issue rather than a scoring-definition issue.
